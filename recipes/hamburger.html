Hamburger

Description

The best burger recipe for summertime grilling! This juicy burger is jam-packed with all kinds of stuff and has no tasteless bread crumbs.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Seasonings and sauces

Steps

  1. Combine the ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Make four patties.
  3. Grill the patties until the juices run clear and the meat is no longer pink in the center.
  4. Add all stuff inside the cut patties
