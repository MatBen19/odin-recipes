Overnight Protein Oatmeal

Description

To make overnight protein oatmeal, combine ½ cup of rolled oats, 1 cup of milk (or a milk alternative), and 1 scoop of protein powder in a jar or bowl. Stir well to fully mix the protein powder, then add 1 tablespoon of chia seeds and a pinch of cinnamon for extra texture and flavor. Mix in any desired sweetener, such as honey or maple syrup, and optional add-ins like berries or nut butter.

Cover the container and refrigerate overnight, or for at least 6 hours, to allow the oats and chia seeds to absorb the liquid. In the morning, give it a good stir, add extra toppings if you like, and enjoy a ready-to-eat, high-protein breakfast straight from the fridge.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • 1 scoop protein powder (flavor of choice)
  • 1 cup milk or plant-based milk
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 pinch cinnamon (optional)
  • 1–2 teaspoons honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • Optional toppings: fresh berries, sliced banana, nut butter, nuts, or seeds

Steps

  1. Add oats, protein powder, milk, chia seeds, cinnamon, and sweetener to a jar or bowl.
  2. Stir until all ingredients are well combined and no protein powder lumps remain.
  3. Cover the jar/bowl with a lid or plastic wrap.
  4. Refrigerate overnight (or at least 6 hours) to let oats soften and flavors develop.
  5. In the morning, stir again and adjust consistency with a splash of milk if needed.
  6. Add your favorite toppings and enjoy.
